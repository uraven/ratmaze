About the Good Rat and Raven

The Good Rat and the Raven are the same person – Peter Bockenthien.



There's more to this guy than meets the eye. He's a very good rat and excellent raven. His analytical gifts lead him 3 steps ahead of confusion. He's the guy you want to follow, and not on Twitter.

His parents gave this middle child a middle initial `E`. His brother and sister got middle names. It took a long time to settle on what he wanted it to stand for – Encourage.

His attitude is mostly positive because he has a gruesome obsession with sticking around to see what happens. He likes going out on a limb because that's where the fruit is, that's where the most amount of learning happens.

He uses his Analytical gifts to develop strategies and tactics. Currently he fights for parents 1st, 9th, and 14th amendment rights.
